# Intelligent Lead Management CRM

**Tech Stack:** Next.js + Express.js

## 📌 Project Overview

Intelligent Lead Management CRM is a system designed for service-based businesses that rely on paid advertising (Facebook, Google, TikTok, etc.) to generate leads.

The platform automatically receives leads from ad campaigns or manual entry, distributes them intelligently to sales agents, and enforces response-time accountability to ensure fast customer follow-up.

The core strength of the system is that it connects:

- Ad spend
- Sales team performance
- Actual customer visits
- Revenue

It can automatically reduce campaign budgets when leads are not contacted within the defined time and restore them once compliance is achieved, helping businesses protect and optimize their advertising investment.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Improve lead response time
- Increase sales team accountability
- Protect advertising budgets
- Accurately measure cost per visit and cost per customer
- Calculate real ROI (Return on Investment)

## 🏢 Target Market

Any service-based business that generates leads through paid ads, including:

- Medical & Dental Clinics
- Real Estate Agencies
- Education & Training Centers
- Fitness & Wellness Businesses
- Beauty Salons
- Legal Firms
- Consulting Services

## 🚀 Vision

To build a smart CRM that goes beyond simple lead management by fully connecting marketing, sales, and revenue performance within one scalable platform.
